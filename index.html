Who am I?

My name is Cauã Magalhães, I am 19 years old, and I am a programming student. My interest in technology was strongly influenced by my family, and over time, I developed a genuine passion for learning and understanding more about the world of programming.

I am still at the beginning of my career, so I do not have a long professional background yet. However, I am fully committed to this learning journey. After some personal experiences in my life, I realized that programming has always been my true vocation. I simply had not been in the right environment or surrounded by the right influences before.

Today, I am working hard to achieve my dream of becoming a skilled programmer, capable of solving problems, building meaningful projects, and constantly improving my knowledge. Since I was younger, I have always been curious about technology, but I did not dedicate myself to it as much as I should have. Now, at 19 years old, I want to do things differently and turn this dream into reality.

I consider myself a very curious person when it comes to programming. I always try to learn something new every day, seek guidance from people who already have experience in the field, and explore topics I am not familiar with so I can build a more diverse and solid foundation.

I can be a little introverted when I am in a new environment, but once I become comfortable, I turn into a very communicative person who enjoys building good relationships with others.

Currently, I am studying JavaScript and its libraries, focusing on becoming a front-end developer and specializing in this area. I also plan to study back-end development in the future, but for now, my main focus is front-end development because it is the area where I feel most comfortable and connected.
